Bakers bin

The history of Bakers Bin began in 1999 with its first store in Pretoria. Founded by Marius Erasmus, the business was built on the vision of making baking accessible to everyone from beginners to professionals by offering a wide variety of ingredients and tools at affordable prices.

  • Mission
    • -To be the ultimate one-stop-shop for bakers, ensuring quality, variety, and expert support in every branch.
  • Vision
    • -To make the art of baking easy and enjoyable for everyone, from home hobbyists to professional pastry chefs.
